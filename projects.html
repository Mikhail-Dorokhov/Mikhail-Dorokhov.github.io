Automating the lights on my mouse

I recently was forced to replace my mouse. I chose the Steelseries Rival 100. So far my only complaint is that there is no button on the mouse to control the built in lights. To solve this, I decided to create an automated program to turn the lights on in the morning, and turn them off at night.
